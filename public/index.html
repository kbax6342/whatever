Creating a space for our community to be valued, appreciated and celebrated has always been the goal of the big man movement. Thabigdogs, Heetizm and Carolina Beef decided to take this movement to the next level and created Royal Flush in Vegas. This is 4 days of brotherhood, celebration, networking and love! So come with friends or come to meet some new ones. Come to experience life, fun and laughs and let go of the everyday world for 4 days.

Two Bedroom Suite

  • The da Vinci Suite (Two Bedroom)
  • Premier Two Bedroom Suite

Our expansive Two Bedroom Suites feature a split floor plan with a large, central living room flanked by a bedroom on each end. Each of the dual master bedrooms has its own full bathroom, one of which features a jetted tub. With over 1,300 sq. ft. of space, the Two Bedroom Suite can comfortably accommodate up to six overnight guests.

Whether visiting for business or pleasure, the Two Bedroom Suite is the perfect place to gather. A wet bar runs the length of the living room, equipped with a refrigerator, microwave, and coffee maker. A long boardroom table makes this beautiful suite the perfect gathering place. Plenty of comfortable seating surrounds a wall-mounted flat screen television in the living room.
